MEET YOUR CAREER COACH

Dacia DeRidder is a seasoned career coach with 30 years of corporate experience, having led HR and customer experience initiatives at global organizations such as Google, Gallagher, and Analyte Health.

Her career has spanned the globe, with professional experience in countries like India, China, the UK, South Korea, and Brazil, providing her with a unique perspective and deep understanding of diverse work environments.

DeRidder holds an MBA from Loyola University of Chicago, USA, and executive HR certifications including SHRM-SCP and DE&I. She is a member of the Institute of Coaching (IOC) and the National Resume Writers Association (NRWA), and she is a Nationally Certified Online Profile Expert™ (NCOPE).

As the founder of MissFit Coaching, she leverages her extensive corporate expertise and deep knowledge of organizational dynamics to empower professionals in their career journeys.

Her work with individuals across various industries and backgrounds has enabled countless professionals to take control of their careers, secure jobs they love, step into leadership roles, and navigate career transitions successfully—without relying on luck.

DeRidder is driven by the belief that everyone deserves a fulfilling career.

Her coaching approach is rooted in empathy, authenticity, and a comprehensive understanding of the challenges and opportunities at every career stage, fostering unstoppable career growth for her clients.
