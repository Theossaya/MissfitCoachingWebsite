IT DOESN’T HAVE TO BE THIS WAY

Ever feel like your career is just... stuck?

Maybe it’s a promotion that never came, a layoff you didn’t see coming, or a project that didn’t go anywhere. Suddenly, you’re left wondering, “What now?”

The truth is most of us are waiting. Waiting for someone else to notice us. Waiting for a recruiter to call. Waiting for the next big opportunity to magically appear.

But waiting doesn’t work.

And when nothing changes, the frustration starts to build.

You tell yourself to hang in there. One more project. One more networking event.

But deep down, you’re asking yourself:

“Am I stuck? What if this is far as I go?

Then the doubts start to creep in:

  • “Am I even relevant anymore?”
  • “Should I settle for something I don’t want to just to keep the lights on?”
  • “Will I ever find something that excites me again?

A BETTER WAY

The good news is that you don’t have to live like this any longer.

You don’t have to wait for that fabled “Big break.”

You can take control of your career right now.

Imagine a coaching program that:

  • Is tailored to your goals—whether you want to secure a promotion, switch industries, or navigate career transitions—without relying on vague advice or random opportunities.
  • Supports you in taking consistent, actionable steps toward achieving your career milestones and reaching your full potential.
  • Helps you build confidence in your abilities, manage your career strategically, and gain the skills needed to create the professional future you want.
  • Gives you the tools, strategies, and accountability to accelerate your career.
  • Helps you build a strong, compelling personal brand that your teammates, superiors, and employers can’t ignore.

MEET YOUR CAREER COACH

Dacia DeRidder is a seasoned career coach with 30 years of corporate experience, having led HR and customer experience initiatives at global organizations such as Google, Gallagher, and Analyte Health.

Her career has spanned the globe, with professional experience in countries like India, China, the UK, South Korea, and Brazil, providing her with a unique perspective and deep understanding of diverse work environments.

DeRidder holds an MBA from Loyola University of Chicago, USA, and executive HR certifications including SHRM-SCP and DE&I. She is a member of the Institute of Coaching (IOC) and the National Resume Writers Association (NRWA), and she is a Nationally Certified Online Profile Expert™ (NCOPE).

As the founder of MissFit Coaching, she leverages her extensive corporate expertise and deep knowledge of organizational dynamics to empower professionals in their career journeys.

Her work with individuals across various industries and backgrounds has enabled countless professionals to take control of their careers, secure jobs they love, step into leadership roles, and navigate career transitions successfully—without relying on luck.

DeRidder is driven by the belief that everyone deserves a fulfilling career.

Her coaching approach is rooted in empathy, authenticity, and a comprehensive understanding of the challenges and opportunities at every career stage, fostering unstoppable career growth for her clients.
